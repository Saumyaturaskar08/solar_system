Earth: Our Home Planet

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life. It has a diverse environment with vast oceans, forests, and an atmosphere that sustains living organisms.

Key Facts:

  • Diameter: 12,742 km
  • Orbit Period: 365.25 Earth days
  • Surface Temperature: -88°C to 58°C
  • Distance from the Sun: 149.6 million km
