Pluto: The Dwarf Planet

Pluto is a dwarf planet located in the Kuiper Belt, a region beyond Neptune. Once considered the ninth planet of our solar system, it was reclassified as a dwarf planet in 2006. Pluto has a complex surface with mountains, plains, and a thin atmosphere.
