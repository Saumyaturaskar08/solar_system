Mercury: The Swift Planet

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and the closest to the Sun. It completes an orbit around the Sun in just 88 Earth days. Despite being so close, its temperature varies widely between day and night.

Key Facts:

  • Diameter: 4,880 km
  • Orbit Period: 88 Earth days
  • Temperature Range: -173°C to 427°C
  • Distance from the Sun: 57.9 million km
