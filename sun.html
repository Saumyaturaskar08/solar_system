The Sun: The Heart of Our Solar System

The Sun is the center of our solar system and the source of energy that sustains life on Earth. It's a giant ball of hot gases, mainly hydrogen and helium, with immense gravity that holds the solar system together.

Key Facts:

  • Age: 4.6 billion years
  • Diameter: 1.39 million km
  • Surface Temperature: 5,500°C
  • Core Temperature: 15 million°C
  • Distance from Earth: 149.6 million km
